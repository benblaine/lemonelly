FAQ

Common questions

Nothing's free. What's the catch?

There isn't one. We carry the cost of the draft because most people who see their finished site want to keep it. If you don't, you walk away and owe nothing.

What does a website cost with lemonelly?

Nothing upfront. The design, the draft, and every revision are free. You only start paying £95/month once you're happy and your new site goes live.

How fast will my site be live?

Seven days from kickoff. Days 1–2 we design your free draft, days 3–5 we refine it with your feedback, days 6–7 we go live.

What does the £95/month actually cover?

Everything: fast hosting, SSL security, software and security updates, one round of content edits every month — new photos, price changes, fresh copy — plus SEO and direct WhatsApp support. No upfront cost. No hidden fees.

What do you need from me to get started?

Just tell us about your business and point us at your current website if you have one. We take it from there and review everything with you before launch.

Can I change things after the site is live?

Yes. One round of updates is included every single month, so your site keeps up as your business grows — just send us what's changed.
